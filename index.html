Who Are We?

Located on the beautiful ocean frontage in the heart of Hubbards, Nova Scotia, South Shore Board Sports has the best services for all your stand-up paddle surfing needs. Whether you are looking to purchase or rent equipment, or experience our fantastic SUP yoga lessons, we are here to provide affordable and friendly service to all our customers.

What is SUP?

Stand-up paddle surfing and stand-up paddle boarding (Hoe he'e nalu in the Hawaiian Language) originated in Hawaii as an offshoot of surfing; however, they have existed in some form for thousands of years. Unlike traditional surfing where the rider sits until a wave comes, stand up-paddle boarders stand upright and use a paddle to propel themselves through the water.
